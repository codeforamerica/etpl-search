Description

The objective of the program is to prepare students to obtain QuickBooks end-user certification. The program is intended to broaden, deepen and verify your knowledge of QuickBooks Financial Software, and demonstrate your QuickBooks capabilities to current and future employers. Students will also prepare for Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) certification which can help you differentiate yourself in today's competitive job market, broaden your employment opportunities by displaying your advanced skills, and resulting in higher earning potential. This program consists of the following:The Microsoft Word 2013 course prepares students in creating, customizing, organizing, formatting. using visual content, reviewing, sharing, and securing contents of documents.The Microsoft Excel 2013 course prepares students in creating, manipulating and formatting data, formulas, presenting data visually, and securing data in worksheets.The Microsoft Access 2013 course prepares students in structuring, creating and formatting database elements, creating and modifying queries, and entering, sharing and modifying data.The Microsoft Outlook 2013 course prepares students in managing messaging, schedules and tasks, contacts, and organizing information.The Microsoft PowerPoint 2013 course prepares students in creating and formatting presentation masters and templates, slide content, dynamic visual content and presentations.The QuickBooks Comprehensive course focuses on using lntuit's QuickBooks program to perform comprehensive business accounting functions.Graduates will obtain the skill set and gain the knowledge necessary to pass the Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) Certification exams, and the QuickBooks end-user certification exam.Ideal career tracks for this course: Executive Assistant, Office Administrator, Business Entrepreneur/Consultant, Receptionist, Medical Office Assistant, Accounts Receivable/Accounts Payable Clerk, etc.
