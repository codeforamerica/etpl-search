Description

The Public Relations program will complement the Communications, Journalism and TV Production tracks available in the Arts and Humanities division. The Public Relations program will allow students to learn how todevelop relationships with diverse publics through a values-driven approach. It will also provide students the opportunity to effectively communicate complex messages through a variety of mediums.AA programs are primarily designed for students who plan to transfer as juniors at four year colleges and universities. CCC graduates have obtained bachelor's degrees and beyond from every college in New Jersey and scores ofcolleges and universities throughout America. Cumberland has transfer agreements with a number of four-year colleges and universities. As a graduate of a fully accreditedcommunity college, your coursework will be received with full credit transfer at most state colleges, public and private universities across the country. The NJ Lampitt bill passed in 2008 by the NJ State legislature assures seamlesstransfer of credits toward junior standing at NJ state colleges and universities.
